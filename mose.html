MOVIES & SERIES

Marvel movies and series are part of the interconnected Marvel Cinematic Universe (MCU) and can be watched in either release order or chronological order to experience the grand story. Key films include Iron Man, The Avengers, Black Panther, and Avengers: Endgame, while major series like WandaVision, Loki, and The Falcon and the Winter Soldier expand the universe, especially in the "Multiverse Saga" which includes many of the more recent releases.
