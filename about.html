About Us


Marvel Insight is a website developed from scratch by two FEU Alabang students, Clare Eala and Jericho De Guzman of section GAS 1201A, with the goal of becoming a hub of information on all things Marvel. Clare and Jericho share a deep love for the Marvel Cinematic Universe and sought a place where every piece of lore can be found. This website was created as their final performance task for the subject Introduction to Mechatronics.

All information available in Marvel Insight is sourced from various public resources on the web. All information and images were taken from Marvelfandom.com, Wikipedia, the official Marvel website, and Google. All credits to the original owners. Refer to the Marvel Database: Copyrights page for additional information.

This website was created with HTML and CSS in 2025, published on the web using GitHub.
